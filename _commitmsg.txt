Chronological Stage 1 fixes: blank-on-enter + remember canonical spot

- Switching to chronological while already on a passage's start chapter
  (e.g. Genesis 1) showed a blank page: pickPassage cleared the verses but
  the chapter loader didn't refire because book/chapter didn't change. Drop
  the manual clearing — the loader already clears and reloads on a real
  book/chapter change, so an unchanged target just keeps its verses.
- Flipping back to Canonical stranded you on the chronological passage's
  book/chapter. Now entering chronological stashes your canonical spot and
  leaving restores it.
